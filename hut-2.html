Details

The second Hidden Hut (or should we say a pod!) at Berry Farm is fully decked out for all your needs, and has a quirky charm. The kitchen is situated outside and has cold running water as is the flushing loo, don't worry though it's totally private! The shared showers, with hot running water, are a 5 min walk away.

Unfortunately, this pod is not suitable for children and pets due to its accessibility.
